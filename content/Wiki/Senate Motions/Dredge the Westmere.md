Motion to [[Dredge the Westmere|dredge the Westmere]] at Meade March, [[Mitwold]]. This [[Commission|commission]] [[Will|will]] result in the creation of 2 ministries. The first, a [[Mithril|mithril]] [[Ministry|ministry]] in Meade, to be held by the '[[Alderman]] of Meade' liaison to the Tseriyn [[Family|family]] of [[Faraden]]. This would be an annually elected Marcher [[Bourse]] position. The second, an [[Ilium|ilium]] [[Ministry|ministry]] in Fisk, [[Kallavesa]], to be held by the 'Financier of Fisk'. Both to be appointed by the tally of votes. We have secured funding.
## Overview
* Creates a Ministry in [[Mitwold]] and a ministry in [[Kallavesa]] as per the [[Butterfly in reverse]] wind of fortune
* Passed

## Outcome
* Once complete, the position of ''Alderman of Meade'' [[Will|will]] be available to elect by [[Tally of the Votes]] by the owners of farms in [[The Marches]] and the position of ''Financier of Fisk'' will be available to elect by [[Tally of the Votes]] by the commanders of military units in [[Wintermark]]
* Completion of this [[Commission|commission]] will add the Coastal quality to Meade March in Mitwold and it will see an overall seasonal increase of nine thrones in taxation from [[Bregasland]], Kallavesa, and Mitwold
