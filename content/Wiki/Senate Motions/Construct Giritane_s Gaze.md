I Sagua i'Ezmara, [[Master of the Koboldi]], am hereby announcing the construction of Giritane's Gaze, a [[Great work|great work]] supporting miners in [[Weirwater]]. It [[Will|will]] take the form of a large white mandowla statue with glowing eyes. [[Enchanter]] Baldwin, [[Senator|senator]] of [[Weirwater]], has agreed to provide the funding.

## Outcome
* Builds an immense white stone statue of a Mandowla to harness the power of a [[Summer]] [[Regio]], in Wickmoor on top a hill to the northwest of the [[Weirwater Vales]].
* Plans include the use of two massive lightstones which [[Will|will]] make the colossus especially visible at [[Night|night]] 
* Now completed, this ''Alabaster Owlbeast'' provides every [[Mine]] in [[Weirwater]] with a share in a bounty of 900 rings, representing precious metals and gemstones hewn from the earth alongside the magical [[Materials|materials]] the mines also produce.
