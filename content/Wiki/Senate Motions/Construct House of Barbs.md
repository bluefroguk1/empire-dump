### Overview
* Passed at 1 ring
* To be constructed in Hercynia.
* The [[Sinecure]] [[Will|will]] provide 12 votes in the [[Imperial Synod]] to the ''[[Voice of Barbs]]''.

* [[Spring]] 379YE

### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The House of Barbs is now complete
* The Voice of Barbs must now be appointed
* After a false start earlier in the year, [[The Senate|the Senate]] commissioned an extension to the House of Barbs in [[Summer]] 382YE that increase the amount of [[Liao|liao]] and votes provided by the sinecure.
### Constitution
* Sinecures are normally life-time appointments. 
* The title "Voice of Barbs" provides votes in the [[Imperial Synod]], and so grants the holder of that title the legal powers and responsibilities of membership of the [[Synod]]. If the holder of the title is not already a [[Member of the Synod|member of the Synod]], it automatically includes membership of all appropriate assemblies (usually the [[General]] [[Assembly]], the Assembly of [[The Way|the Way]], and the appropriate [[National Assembly]]). 
* In future, any [[Senator]] submitting a motion of equivalent length or verbosity [[Will|will]] be advised to revise it before the motion will be accepted by a speaker.
