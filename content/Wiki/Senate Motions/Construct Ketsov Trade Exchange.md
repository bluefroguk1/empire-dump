 An announcement by [[Bearer of an Imperial Wayleave|bearer of an Imperial Wayleave]], Irina Vasileva Dukov. To build a trading exchange in the town of Ketsov in [[Ossium]]. This [[Ministry|ministry]] [[Will|will]] allow the purchase of magical weapons and armour, taking advantage of the [[Opportunity|opportunity]] provided by the people of Ketsov. This [[Will|will]] create the title of [[Burgher of Ketsov]] - a Varushkan National [[Bourse]] position appointed annually by vote of Varushkan [[Mine|mine]] and [[Forest|forest]] owners.
''Announced by [[Bearer of an Imperial Wayleave|bearer of an Imperial Wayleave]]''
