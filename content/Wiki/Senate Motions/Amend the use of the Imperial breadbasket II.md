To permanently change the Imperial Breadbasket to include the Mournwold.
Proposed by Bregasland, Seconded by Skarsind. 
### Overview
* This motion was to [[Amend the use of the Imperial breadbasket|further amend]] the use of the [[The Marches economic interests#Granaries and Storehouses - the Imperial Breadbasket|Imperial Breadbasket]].
* As outlined [[An apple that falls#Sure Of Having Something|here]], this motion would have allowed the benefits of the Imperial Breadbasket to spread to the Mournwold and increase in effectiveness, but would have permanently removed the ability of the breadbasket to support additional Imperial armies. 
* Failed
