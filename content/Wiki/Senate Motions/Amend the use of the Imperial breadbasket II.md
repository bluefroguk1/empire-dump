To permanently change the Imperial Breadbasket to include the [[Mournwold]].
Proposed by [[Bregasland]], Seconded by [[Skarsind]]. 
### Overview
* This motion was to further [[Amend the use of the Imperial breadbasket|amend the use of the Imperial Breadbasket]].
* As outlined here, this motion would have allowed the benefits of the Imperial Breadbasket to spread to the [[Mournwold]] and increase in effectiveness, but would have permanently removed the ability of the breadbasket to support additional [[Imperial armies]]. 
* Failed
