[[The Senate]] reasserts that the [[Sungold Pass]] and [[The Mountain|the mountain]] known as the Traveller are part of the region of Opascari, in [[Volodmartz]], and that the exploitation of resources in Imperial Land by foreigners without the direct invitation of [[The Empire|the Empire]] is not acceptable.''Proposed by [[Karov]], seconded by [[Sarvos]].''
## Overview
* The [[Senate]] asserts that the [[Sungold Pass]] is in [[The Empire|the Empire]], rather than in [[Otkodov]].
* Passed.
## Date
* [[Summer]] 382YE
## Outcome
* This has implications for the [[Thule]] who may also have laid claim to the [[Mine|mine]].
* During the Summer Solstice 383YE, the Senate chose to cede Sungold Pass to the [[Thule]], effectively abrogating this motion.
