To reform the [[Bloodcloaks]] [[Wintermark]] Army, a Grimnir (Physick) laden army.''Proposed by [[Hahnmark]], seconded by [[Bregasland]]'' 
### Overview
* Permits the Winterfolk to begin raising a new [[Imperial army]].
* Withdrawn after passage of a motion to allow the Dawnish to raise an army.
### Date
* Spring 379YE
### Costs
* The motion would have had all the standard costs of raising a new army.
### Campaign Outcome
* The Bloodcloaks were originally a Wintermark army, lost to the [[Thule]] during the reign of [[Empress Brannan]]. Known for their crimson cloaks, the army was composed of many grimnir and Mystics; all save a few were slaughtered during a disastrous retreat from [[Otkodov]].
