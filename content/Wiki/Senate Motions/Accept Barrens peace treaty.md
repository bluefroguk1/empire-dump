To accept the terms offered by [[The Barrens|the Barrens]] orcs and humans to move troops through [[The Barrens|the Barrens]] (see attached terms). This does not cover the movement of the Navarri army of the [[Black Thorns]] through the Barrens into Broceliande. This is to happen regardless of these terms.Proposed by [[Upwold]], Seconded by [[Therunin]].
### Overview
* A delegation from The Barrens negotiated a treaty.
* The treaty was presented to [[The Senate|the Senate]].
### Date
* Defeated [[Summer]] 379YE
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The terms of the treaty were not accepted.
* The Barrens delegation had already left [[Anvil]] when this motion was presented and defeated; it is not clear if they are aware their treaty has been declined.
