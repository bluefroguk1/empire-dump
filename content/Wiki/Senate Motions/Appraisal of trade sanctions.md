We request that the civil service [[Conduct|conduct]] an [[Appraisal|appraisal]] of the economic consequences of trade [[Sanctions|sanctions]] against slavery, and ways to mitigate them, agreed at the [[Liberty Pact]] summit affecting trade with [[Jarm]], [[Asavea]], the [[Thule]], the [[Iron Confederacy]], and the [[Faraden]].''Proposed by [[Holberg]], seconded by [[Necropolis]].''
## Overview
* Commissions an [[Appraisal]] from the civil service, relating to the new [[Tariffs]] placed on slaving nations by the Liberty Pact.
* Only one appraisal may be carried out each season.
* Passed
## Date
* [[Winter]] 382YE
## [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The Civil Service prepare a report for [[The Empire|the Empire]] delivered before the [[Spring]] Equinox 383YE. You can find details here.
## Constitution
As the first official Appraisal, there was some irregularity in how this motion, and the other two proposed appraisals, were dealt with. Going forward, while it is likely the Speaker [[Will|will]] arrange a single debate on all appraisals proposed during a session, they [[Will|will]] then be voted on in sequence rather than concurrently.
