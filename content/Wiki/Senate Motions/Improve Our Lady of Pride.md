Announcement by [[Imperial Master of Works]] to improve [[Fortification|fortification]] in [[Sarvos]].''Raised by the [[Imperial Master of Works]].''
## Overview
* This [[Commission|commission]] increases the size of the [[Fortification]] Our Lady of [[Pride]] in [[Sarvos]].
* The improvements [[Will|will]] benefit from vigilant citizens as long as [[Materials|materials]] are provided before the [[Winter]] Solstice.
* Announcement.
## Date
* [[Autumn]] 382YE
## [[Cost]]
* This [[Commission|commission]] uses standard costs.
* 100 wains of [[White granite|White Granite]]
* 200 Crowns of labour.
* One year to complete.
## Progress
* 30 wains of [[White granite|white granite]] and 7 thrones 4 crowns provided by  Gancio after the Autumn Equinox 382YE.
* 20 wains of [[White granite|white granite]] and 40 crowns effectively provided due to the Silent [[Sentinel]] [[Opportunity|opportunity]].
* 25 wains of [[White granite|white granite]] provided by Landon of Longridge after the [[Spring]] Equinox 383YE.
* A further 6 Thrones, 2 Crowns provided by Imperatrix Lisabetta after the Spring Eqinox 383YE.
* 25 wains of [[White granite|white granite]] provided by Lieselotte van [[Holberg]] after [[Summer]] Solstice 383YE.
* 50 crowns provided by Severin Teyhard von [[Holberg]], for Imperatrix Lisabetta after Summer Solstice 383YE.
## [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* When complete, the [[Fortification]] [[Will|will]] be increased to an effective strength of 6,000.
