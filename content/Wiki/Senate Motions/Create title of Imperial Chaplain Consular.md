To appoint an imperial position: [[Imperial Chaplain Consular]]
A [[Synod|synod]] virtue title (of [[The Way|the way]]) to be appointed by the [[Cardinal of the Way]].
Responsibilities: Advise Imperial Ambassadors on matters pertaining to [[The Way|the way]]. Provide Oversight of Imperial Ambassadors for [[The Synod|the Synod]]. Study foreigners to facilitate comparative theology
Powers:Once per Season, may inquist an imperial [[Ambassador|ambassador]] without requiring a [[Judgement]] of the Synod. May spend up to five thrones per season from the Virtue fund.''Proposed by [[Bastion]], seconded by [[Therunin]].''
