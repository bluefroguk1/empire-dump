To raise a fortress in [[Miekarova]] (Self Funded)''Proposed by [[Miekarova]], seconded by [[Karsk]]'' 
### Overview
* The creation of a new [[Fortification]], in the region of Perumaki.
* The [[Fortification|fortification]] [[Will|will]] be named Grimhold.
* The fortress is being built in the Vale of Strascovia, home of the Vor'Azi people, using the crumbling ruins of their ancestral castle as the foundation.
* A previous motion to raise a [[Fortification|fortification]] in this Varushkan [[Territory|territory]] was defeated in [[Winter]] 378YE.
### Date
* [[Spring]] 379YE
### Costs
* [[Materials]]: 80 [[White granite|white granite]] and 20 Thrones. (Standard costs).
* Time: 1 year to construct
* Upkeep: 10 Thrones per season.
### Progress
* 40 wains of [[White granite|white granite]] and 20 thrones provided by Dušan Otecovna Zlata after Spring Equinox 379YE.
* 40 wains of [[White granite|white granite]] provided by Dušan Otecovna Zlata after [[Autumn]] Equinox 379YE.
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* When complete, this [[Will|will]] create a rank one [[Fortification|fortification]] in [[Miekarova]].
