To abrogate the ‘favoured nation’ trading status of the [[Principalities of Jarm]]. In [[Autumn]] 380YE, the [[Imperial Senate]] passed the following motion: ‘[[The Empire]] recognizes the importance of extant and future trade in strategic [[Materials|materials]] with our allies in [[Jarm]], and welcomes any overtures to strengthen existing agreements or form new ones.’ With Jarm no longer pursuing it’s previous worthy efforts to rid itself of the blight of slavery, [[The Empire|the Empire]] abrogates this motion.''
Proposed by [[Kallavesa]], seconded by [[Segura]].''
### Overview
* The motion referred to that passed during the Autumn Equinox 380YE, recognising the [[Principalities of Jarm]] as favoured trading partners.
* Passed
### Date
* Autumn 382YE
### Outcome
* Lord-[[Magister]] Anton Trescher, who advises the House of Princes on matters of international trade, has been informed of [[The Senate|the Senate]]'s decision.
* This [[Will|will]] end favoured trade status with Jarm, likely resulting in the loss of additional crystal mana recieved by ship captains trading with the Principalities.
