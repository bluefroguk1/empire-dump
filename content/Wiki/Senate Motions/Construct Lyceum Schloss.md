To permit the [[Commonwealth]] to build a schloss on the island east of Siroc - [[Madruga]] and to cede the schloss to [[The Commonwealth|the Commonwealth]]
## Overview
* Authorises the [[Commonwealth]] to construct a schloss for their citizens who are visiting the Lyceum and cedes the structure to the Commonwealth. 
* This construction does not count towards the limit on commissions.
* Passed
## Date
* [[Winter]] 383YE
## [[Cost]]
* Non-standard costs apply
* 40 wains of [[White granite|white granite]]
* 10 wains of [[Weirwood|weirwood]]
* 100 crowns in labour costs
## Progress
*12 thrones 4 crowns, 40 wains of [[White granite|white granite]], and 10 wains of [[Weirwood|weirwood]] provided by Militärattache Ludkhannah Schöningen after Winter Solstice 383YE
## Outcome
* Now complete the Lyceum receives a permanent improvement in the speed with which it can codify [[Rituals|rituals]] designed to enchant an army.
