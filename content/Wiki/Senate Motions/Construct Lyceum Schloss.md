To permit the [[Commonwealth]] to build a schloss on the island east of Siroc - [[Madruga]] and to cede the schloss to The the Commonwealth
## Overview
* Authorises the Commonwealth to construct a schloss for their citizens who are Visiting the Lyceum and cedes the structure to the Commonwealth. 
* This construction does not count toWards the limit on Commissions.
* Passed
## Date
* Winter 383YE
## [[Cost]]
* Non-standard costs apply
* 40 wains of [[White granite]]
* 10 wains of [[Weirwood]]
* 100 crowns in labour costs
## PrOgress
*12 thrones 4 crowns, 40 wains of white granite, and 10 wains of weirwood provided by Militärattache Ludkhannah Schöningen after Winter Solstice 383YE
## Outcome
* Now complete the Lyceum receives a permanent improvement in the speed with which it can codify [[Rituals]] designed to enchant an army.
