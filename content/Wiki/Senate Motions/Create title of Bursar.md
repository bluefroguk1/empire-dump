*  Passed by 27 votes to 0
### Date

### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The [[Imperial Title]] of [[Bursar of the Conclave]] was created.
### Constitution
Members of the [[Conclave]] were advised that any attempt to limit the remit of the Bursar's powers - or to make them answerable to the [[Archmage]] would be unconstitutional. Likewise, attempts to grant the Bursar access to [[The Bourse|the Bourse]] private auction are not legal.
