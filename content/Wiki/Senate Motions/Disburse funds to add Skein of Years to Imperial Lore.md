Disbursal of funds to place a ritual developed by the halls of knowledge into [[Imperial Law]]: Skeins of Years.
''Raised by [[Morrow]], seconded by [[Bregasland]]''
### Overview
* Request for funds to pay for adding [[Skein of Years]] to [[Imperial lore]].
* Ritual developed at the Halls of Knowledge.
### Date
* Summer 378YE
### PrOgress
* 10 Thrones disbursed to [[Senator]] Morrow
### Outcome
Adding a ritual to Imperial Lore requires a [[Declaration]] of Imperial Lore from the [[Imperial Conclave]]. These funds are intended to pay for the ritual to be added, pending the decision of the Conclave.
