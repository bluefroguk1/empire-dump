__NOTOC__
[[The Empire]] recognises the importance of extant and future trade in strategic [[Materials|materials]] with our allies in [[Jarm]] and welcomes any overtures to strengthen existing agreements or form new ones.
Proposed by [[Upwold]], Seconded by [[Morrow]]
### Overview
* Passed
### Date
* [[Autumn]] 380YE
### Outcome
* The motion secured favoured trading status for the [[Principalities of Jarm]], resulting in an increased amount of crystal mana available on the docks.
* The motion was later abrogated during the Autumn Equinox 382YE.
