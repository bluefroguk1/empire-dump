To invite an [[Archmage|archmage]] of [[The Conclave|the conclave]] to address us on the activities of the [[Conclave|conclave]] and how they can assist [[The Empire|the empire]].Proposed by [[Kallavesa]], seconded by [[Sermersuaq]]. 
### Overview
* Passed 25 votes to 3.
### Date
* Passed [[Autumn]] 377YE.
### Progress
* Savik Summerstorm, [[Archmage of Winter]], addressed [[The Senate|the Senate]] Summit of 377YE.
### Constitution
It is very rare for it to be deemed constitutional for the [[Senate]] to have the power to compel an Imperial citizen to appear before them, that power more normally resides with the [[Imperial Synod]], but they can grant the power of address to an Imperial citizen, either on an ongoing basis or to be used once.
