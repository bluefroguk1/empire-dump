__NOTOC__
To grant the Orcs of the [[Great Forest of Peytaht]] the status of foreigners
Proposed by [[Casinea]], Seconded by [[Necropolis]]
### Overview
* Under [[Imperial Law]], this motion grants all the legal protections and responsiblities of a foreigner to the orcs of the Great Forest of Peytaht.
* This follows on from two previous motions, the first to declare peace, the second to declare [[War]] on the collective orcs of [[The Barrens]]
### Date
* Summer 380YE
### Campaign Outcome
* The [[Declaration]] means that the orcs of the Great Forest of Peytaht are foreigners under Imperial Law
