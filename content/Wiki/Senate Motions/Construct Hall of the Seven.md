__NOTOC__
To construct the Hall of the Seven in [[Miaren]]. This creates the position of [[Spear of the Seven]], a military counterpart to the [[Advisor on the Vallorn]]. Elected annually by [[Navarr]] senators, for [[Revocation|revocation]] by National or [[Imperial Synod]].
## Overview
* Creates a [[Ministry]] in [[Miaren]], as detailed in the [[The bones of what you believe]] wind of fortune
* Announcement by the Bearer of the First Imperial Wayleave
## Date
* [[Spring]] 385YE
## [[Cost]]
* Costs as per wind of fortune
* 10 wains of [[Weirwood|weirwood]]
* 10 wains of [[Mithril|mithril]]
* 5 thrones in labour costs
* Three months to construct
