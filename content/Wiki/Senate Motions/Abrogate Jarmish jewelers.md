Abrogate the Jarmish jewelers passed in [[Autumn]] 382YE.''Proposed by [[Mournwold]], seconded by [[Upwold]].''
### Overview
* The motion referred to that passed during the Autumn Equinox 382YE, commissioning the construction of a jewelers premises for the Kobarid [[Family|family]] of [[Jarm]].
* Passed
### Date
* [[Winter]] 382YE
### Outcome
* Construction of the jewelers premises has been halted. A later motion was raised before [[The Senate|the Senate]] to resume construction but was defeated.
