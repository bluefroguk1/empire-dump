Abrogate the Jarmish jewelers passed in Autumn 382YE.''Proposed by Mournwold, seconded by Upwold.''
### Overview
* The motion referred to that passed during the Autumn Equinox 382YE, commissioning the [[Construct Jarmish Jewellers|construction of a jewelers premises]] for the Kobarid family of Jarm.
* Passed
### Date
* Winter 382YE
### Outcome
* Construction of the jewelers premises has been halted. A [[Construct Kobarid Jewelers|later motion]] was raised before the Senate to resume construction but was defeated.
