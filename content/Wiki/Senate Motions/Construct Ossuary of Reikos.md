For the creation of an ossuary in [[Reikos]], a [[Great work|great work]] that [[Will|will]] enable [[The Empire|the Empire]] to lay thousands of highborn dead to rest and enable the rebuilding of the [[Territory|territory]]. This [[Will|will]] increase the returns to the imperial territory and is proposed on the virtue of [[Prosperity|prosperity]].
''Proposed by [[Necropolis]], Seconded by [[Reikos]]'' 
### Overview
* A [[Great work]] in the territory of [[Reikos]]
* Passed
### Date
* [[Spring]] 380YE
### [[Cost]]
* 40 wains of [[White granite|White Granite]]
* 40 wains of [[Weirwood]]
* 20 Thrones in labour costs
* Time : 1 year to construct
### Progress
* 40 wains of [[White granite|white granite]], 40 wains of [[Weirwood|weirwood]], 20 thrones provided by Ianthe after Spring Equinox 380YE.
