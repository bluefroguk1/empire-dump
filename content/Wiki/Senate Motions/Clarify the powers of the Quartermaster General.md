### Overview
* The [[Imperial Title]] of [[Quartermaster General of the Imperial Armies]] was created by the Senate at the previous summit. The civil service raised a procedural motion to clarify the powers and [[Appointment]] of the Quartermaster.
### Date
* Winter 377YE
### Constitution
* The Quartermaster General has the power to [[Resupply Imperial Armies]], they do not have the ability to raise new armies, or to construct or repair fortifications.
* The title is appointed by the Military Council, not the [[Imperial Senate]].
