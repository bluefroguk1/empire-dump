 To [[Ratify treaty with the Iron Confederacy|ratify treaty with the Iron Confederacy]]. That the [[Iron Confederacy]] agrees to step down trade embargo to [[Sanction|sanction]]. In return for [[The Empire|the Empire]] instructing the [[Magistrates|magistrates]] to co-operate with the Suranni templars in tracking down members of the Hand of Dumon.
## Overview
* The motion presents a treaty negotiated by the [[Ambassador to the Iron Confederacy]].
* The treaty has two provisions: the [[Iron Confederacy]] [[Will|will]] remove the embargo on trade with [[The Empire|the Empire]], replacing it with [[Sanctions|sanctions]].
* The Imperial magistrates [[Will|will]] cooperate with Suranni templars in tracking down members of the illegal Hand of Dumon.
* Passed
## Date
* [[Autumn]] 384YE.
## Outcome
* The treaty has been enacted by the Civil Service; new trade status is dependant on the Iron Confederacy also ratifying the treaty
* The treaty is subject to some additional [[Scrutiny]] as the Imperial magistrates determine the best way to cooperate with the Suranni templars.
