__NOTOC__
 To Ratify treaty with the [[Ratify treaty with the Iron Confederacy]]. That the [[Iron Confederacy]] agrees to step down trade embargo to [[Sanction]]. In return for [[The Empire]] instructing the magistrates to co-operate with the Suranni templars in tracking down members of the Hand of Dumon.
## Overview
* The motion presents a treaty negotiated by the [[Ambassador to the Iron Confederacy]].
* The treaty has two proVisions: the Iron Confederacy will remove the embargo on trade with the Empire, replacing it with [[Sanctions]].
* The Imperial magistrates will cooperate with Suranni templars in tracking down members of the illegal Hand of Dumon.
* Passed
## Date
* Autumn 384YE.
## Outcome
* The treaty has been enacted by the Civil Service; new trade status is dependant on the Iron Confederacy also ratifying the treaty
* The treaty is subject to some additional [[Scrutiny]] as the Imperial magistrates deterMine the best way to cooperate with the Suranni templars.
