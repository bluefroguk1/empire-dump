Motion: Under the premise that the [[Imperial Constitution]] is not intended to disadvantage [[The Empire|the Empire]], this motion creates [[The Senate|the Senate]] power of Escalation.
When an election is announced in [[Senate]], the proposing [[Senator]] may call for escalation, the motion [[Will|will]] subsequently become a Constitutional motion and the election [[Will|will]] be immediately triggered.
Any [[Senator|senator]] on the senate floor may propose a candidate (including in absentia), however for a candidate to be appointed they just receive the support of a constitutional majority (including ratification from [[The Throne|the Throne]].)
Should no candidates be elected, a new motion to appoint would need to be submitted.Proposed [[Weirwater]], seconded [[Morrow]]. 
## Overview
* To grant the Senate a new power, that of escalation.
* This was a constitutional vote.
* Passed with constitutional majority.
* Ratified by [[The Throne]].

* [[Winter]] 381YE
## [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The new power of escalation was created.
