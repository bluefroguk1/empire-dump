__NOTOC__
 I, Sagua i'Ezmara [[Master of the Koboldi]], [[Will|will]] be building a glorious statue celebrating the victories in [[Semmerholm]], [[Ossium]] and the wider Empire achieved alongside the [[Knights of Glory]] and to honour our long standing relationship with [[Eleonaris]]. This has been explicitly requested by [[The Conclave|the Conclave]] as a matter of urgency. It [[Will|will]] be placed at the Semmeholm end of the Golden Causeway, where those who cross it can be best appreciate it. It [[Will|will]] then be the decision of [[The Senate|the senate]] to cede the [[Folly|folly]] or retain ownership. As the [[Master of the Koboldi|master of the Koboldi]] [[Will|will]] be busy installing the Runic Hearth of [[Adamant]] the responsibility for warm Dawnish hospitality of the koboldi [[Will|will]] be assumed by Amica Peddler, [[Proxy|proxy]] [[Senator|senator]] of [[Semmerholm]].
