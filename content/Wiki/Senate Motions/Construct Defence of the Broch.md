* Construct the Defence of the Broch, taking advantage of the [[Fall of Brocéliande#The Defence of the Broch|opportunity presented by]] the people of the steadings.
* Passed at 150 thrones

## Cost

* 40 wains of [[Weirwood]]
* 80 crowns labour cost
* Three months to construct
