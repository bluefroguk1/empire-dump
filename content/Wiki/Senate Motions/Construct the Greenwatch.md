To [[Construct the Greenwatch|construct the Greenwatch]] in Casinea to create the national title of 'Greenwatcher' annually appointed by the Senators of Highguard. This building creates a ministry to strengthen those who fight the vallorn, especially those who need to venture into the vallorn miasma. With all responsibilities as required by Ossegrahn, the Rain Lord. Self funded and time limited to this summit.
