[[Senate motion|Senate Motion]]
To amend the motion to create the title of [[Crystal Architect of the Spires]], which was passed at the [[Spring]] Equinox 379YE, to limit the number of commissions they may authorise to one per summit.
[[Administrative motion|Administrative Motion]] 
### Overview
* This motion was part of a larger series of motions to amend the powers of the titles created by [[The Senate|the Senate]] which have the power of [[Commission|commission]], following on from the recent understanding of the limitations of the Imperial Surveyors. 
* Passed
### Date
* Spring 380YE
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The Crystal [[Architect]] of the [[Spires]] may only authorise one [[Commission|commission]] each summit.
