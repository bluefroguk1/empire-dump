__NOTOC__
To create the title of ‘[[Voice of Liberty]]’. This title [[Will|will]] have the ability to call meetings of the “International [[Liberty Pact]]”, the ability to negotiate on behalf of [[The Empire|the Empire]] at Liberty Pact meetings in regards to potential amendments to that treaty. This position shall be appointed by [[The Senate|the Senate]] and [[Will|will]] be upheld for the lifetime of the appointed, [[Revocation|revocation]] by the [[General]] [[Assembly]] and the [[Assembly]] of Nine.
''Proposed by [[Skarsind]], seconded by [[Morrow]]''
### Overview
* Create a title within the [[Senate]] as delegate to the International Liberty Pact
* Passed
### Date
* [[Spring]] 383YE
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The [[Imperial Title]] [[Voice of Liberty]] was created
* The title was appointed at the [[Summer]] Solstive 383YE.
* Eska Crowspeaker, called Chainbreaker, was appointed to the title.
