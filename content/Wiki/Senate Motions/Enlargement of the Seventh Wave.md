Enlarge the Highborne army the Seventh Wave.''Proposed by Reikos, seconded by Miaren.''
## Overview
* To enlarge the Seventh Wave army, in line with the [[Opportunity]] presented by the [[A blessing in disguise#Enlargement|gathering of soldiers]] in [[Highguard]].
* Passed in principle.
## Date
* Autumn 382YE
## Cost
* This enlargement uses [[A blessing in disguise#Enlargement|non-standard costs]].
* 100 wains mithril
* 200 crowns
* Six months to complete
## Progress
* 50 wains of mithril and 100 Crowns provided by Cadeus of the Shattered Tower after the Autumn Equinox 382YE.
* 50 wains of mithril, 12 Thrones, and 4 Crowns provided by Cadeus after the Spring Equinox 383YE.
## Campaign Outcome
* The Seventh Wave is now a [[Imperial army#Enlargement|large]] army.
