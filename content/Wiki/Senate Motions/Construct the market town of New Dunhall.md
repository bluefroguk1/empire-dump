 To build a [[Market Town|market town]], New Dunhall, which [[Will|will]] be a [[Sinecure|sinecure]] generating coin. This is to begin to rebuild after the catastrophic loss of Dunhearth Hall into the recent collapse.To be built near the ruins of Dunhall in [[Hahnmark]], and to be looked over by the [[Caretaker of New Dunhall]]. Requires funds.
Proposed by [[Sermersuaq]], Seconded by the Conscience. 
### Overview
* Passed at 30 Thrones.
### Date
* [[Summer]] 381YE
### Progress
* 20 wains of [[White granite|white granite]] and 40 crowns provided by Atte Arrow-Tongue Metsastajason after the Summer Solstice 381YE.

* The [[Market Town|market town]] [[Will|will]] be complete by the time of the [[Autumn]] Equinox 381YE.
