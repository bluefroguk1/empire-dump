## [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* When complete, the great work will provide a bounty of 50 [[Liao|liao]] and 100 [[Synod]] votes spread between every [[Congregation|congregation]] in Bastion. 
* Philemon is absolutely clear that he will not permit that "[[League]] montebanc" anywhere near this grand scheme.
* The hostels are designed to go all along the proposed Blood Red Roads. While this great work can be built at any time it will only provide half the expected benefits above unless the Blood Red Roads or similar are extended to Bastion.
* The hostels provide the full benefits above following the completion of the White Roads shortly before the Winter Solstice 384YE.
