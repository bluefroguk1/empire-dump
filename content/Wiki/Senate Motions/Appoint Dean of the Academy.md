__NOTOC__
### Overview
* First [[Appointment]] of the Dean of [[The Academy]]
* This motion was raised by the civil service as an administrative motion, as it was the first time the position was up for appointment. Any future appointment must be raised as a motion by a member of the Senate.
### Date
* Spring 380YE
### Campaign Outcome
* Faustina Notturno di [[Sarvos]] was appointed as the first Dean of the Academy
