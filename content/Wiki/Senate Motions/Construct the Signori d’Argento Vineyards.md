__NOTOC__
Proposal to create the ‘Signori d’Argento Vineyards’ in Foracci, [[Sarvos]]. A plantation of the most exquisite cultivars of Catazarro wines to be planted at the outskirts of Aversa Estate. The vineyards [[Will|will]] be under the jurisdiction of the ‘Vine Tender of the Signori d’Argento vineyards’, a national [[League]] position appointed by [[The League|the League]] Senators. It [[Will|will]] be a self funded money [[Sinecure|sinecure]] with no upkeep.
Proposed by [[Temeschwar]], Seconded by [[Holberg]]. 
### Overview
* Passed

### Constitution
* This motioned was abrogated by [[The Senate|the Senate]] at the Summers Solstice 381YE, although Brother Dagon of the Shattered Tower, as holder of an Imperial Wayleave later recommissioned it.
