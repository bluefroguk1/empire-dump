 To appoint an [[Ambassador to the Iron Confederacy]].
Proposed by [[Karov]], Seconded by [[Weirwater]].
### Overview
* This motion is to appoint a citizen to the position of [[Ambassador]] to the [[Iron Confederacy]] and to have the Authority to deal with the [[Iron Confederacy]] on behalf of [[The Empire|the Empire]].
* This election was announced at the last [[Senate session|senate session]] of the [[Summer]] summit. Following Senatorial procedure on elections, the election [[Will|will]] occur at the first [[Senate session|senate session]] of the [[Autumn]] Equinox 381YE.
* Passed.
