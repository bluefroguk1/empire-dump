__NOTOC__
  Motion to give the [[Holberg]] [[Guild]] of Engineers the permission to expand their premises. This [[Will|will]] create the [[Imperial Title|Imperial title]] "[[Chair of the Wolf]]". It [[Will|will]] have the power to announce a [[Commission|commission]] that takes place entirely in [[The League|the League]]. This [[Commission|commission]] can be either a [[Great work|great work]], [[Ministry|ministry]], [[Sinecure|sinecure]], or [[Folly|folly]]. This [[Will|will]] be a [[League]] national position appointed by [[Tally of the Votes]].
## Overview
* The motion gives permission to the [[Holberg]] [[Guild]] of Engineers to expand their premises, as detailed in the [[A health to the company]] wind of fortune.
* It also creates the [[Chair of the Wolf]] [[Imperial Title]] in [[The League]].
* As part of the creation of the title, [[The League|the League]] has commissioned four "impressive structures" one in each of their cities.
* Passed
## Date
* [[Autumn]] 384YE.
## Outcome
* Expansion of the [[Holberg]] [[Guild]] of Engineers has begun
* The [[Chair of the Wolf]] title has been created, and [[Will|will]] be appointed by [[Tally of the Votes]] in [[The League]]
* The [[Chair of the Wolf]] is able to [[Commission|commission]] a [[Great work]], [[Ministry]], [[Sinecure]], or [[Folly]], as long as the [[Commission|commission]] takes place entirely within a League [[Territory]]. This [[Will|will]] not count against the limited number of commissions [[The Empire|the Empire]] can undertake each season.
* This differs from the original proposal presented in [[Winter]] 383YE, but is fully endorsed by the [[Guild]] of Engineers.
