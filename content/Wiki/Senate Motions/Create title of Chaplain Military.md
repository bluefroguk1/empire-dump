So that the [[Military Council|military council]] may benefit as [[The Senate|the senate]] does from its Conscience. To create the [[Imperial Title]] of Chaplain Military. The Chaplain Military is an [[Imperial Title|Imperial title]] of [[The Synod|the Synod]]. The Chaplain Military is intended to observe the decisions of [[The Military|the Military]] Council; to speak on behalf of the virtuous; to keep the Generals focused on the virtuous performance of their duties and to represent the [[Synod]] in military matters. Appointed by the [[Judgement|judgement]] of the [[Assembly]] of Nine, to serve a term of one year. The Chaplain Military is a member of the Military Council. The Chaplain Military can be revoked by the [[Assembly]] of Nine, or the [[General]] Assembly. No person may hold the position of Chaplain Military more than once in their lifetime.
Proposed by [[Necropolis]], Seconded by [[Casinea]].
