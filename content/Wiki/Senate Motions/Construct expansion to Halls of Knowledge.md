To expand the Halls of Knowledge to include an [[Autumn]] wing..''Proposed by [[Morrow]], seconded by [[Miekarova]].''
## Overview
* Expand the Halls of Knowledge in Peregro to include a wing dedicated to the research of [[Autumn magic]].
* Passed in principle.
## Date
* [[Summer]] 382YE
## [[Cost]]
* The civil service have determined the costs for this expansion.
* 25 wains of [[Mithril|mithril]]
* 25 wains of [[White granite|white granite]]
* 25 wains of [[Weirwood|weirwood]]
* 150 crowns
* six months to complete
## Progress
* 25 wains of [[Mithril|mithril]], 19 wains of [[Weirwood|weirwood]], 1 wain of [[White granite|white granite]], and 12 thrones provided by Agrippa after the Summer Solstice 382YE.
* Due to the [[Druj]] invasion of [[Morrow]], all progress on this project was lost. Agrippa received a partial refund after the Summer Solstice 382YE.
## [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The [[Druj]] invasion of Morrow resulted in the destruction of the Halls of Knowlege, and ended construction work. The contributor received a partial refund of [[Materials|materials]] and labour costs. This [[Senate|senate]] [[Commission|commission]] cannot now be completed.
* When complete, this would have allowed the [[Provost of the Halls of Knowledge]] the power to research Autumn [[Magic|magic]] at the rate of 15 ranks each season, and allowed them to spend money to enhance the speed of that research.
