Build a new Imperial [[Shipyard]] in [[Madruga]]
''Proposed by [[Sarvos]], seconded by Madruga.''
### Overview
* Construct a Shipyard at Madruga.
* Provides additional wealth to Freeborn Fleets operating from Madruga.
### Date
* Passed Winter 376YE (Easter 2013).
### [[Cost]]
* 35 Wains of [[White granite]]
* 5 Wains of [[Weirwood]]
* Upkeep: 5 Thrones
### PrOgress
* Costs paid by Mirislav, [[Senator]] for Sarvos in the Spring Equinox 2013 downtime
### Production
* Increased wealth for Madrugan shipping
This [[Great work]] means that all Freeborn fleets operating out of Madruga will produce more wealth for their owners. The total benefit will be 720 rings per season - divided between all the fleets which are active in Madruga that season.
