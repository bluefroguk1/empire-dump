Build a new Imperial [[Shipyard|shipyard]] in [[Madruga]]
''Proposed by [[Sarvos]], seconded by [[Madruga]].''
### Overview
* Construct a [[Shipyard]] at Madruga.
* Provides additional wealth to [[Freeborn]] fleets operating from Madruga.
### Date
* Passed [[Winter]] 376YE (Easter 2013).
### [[Cost]]
* 35 Wains of [[White granite|White Granite]]
* 5 Wains of [[Weirwood]]
* Upkeep: 5 Thrones
### Progress
* Costs paid by Mirislav, [[Senator]] for [[Sarvos]] in the [[Spring]] Equinox 2013 [[Downtime|downtime]]
### Production
* Increased wealth for Madrugan shipping
This [[Great work]] means that all Freeborn fleets operating out of Madruga [[Will|will]] produce more wealth for their owners. The total benefit [[Will|will]] be 720 rings per season - divided between all the fleets which are active in Madruga that season.
