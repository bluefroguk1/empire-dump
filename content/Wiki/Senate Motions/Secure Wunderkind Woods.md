__NOTOC__
A motion to secure the Wunderkind Woods recognising the Rebeshi Players as Guardians of the Wood. This creates the [[Imperial Title|Imperial title]] "[[Tender of the Blood Orchard]]" to be appointed annually by the Senators of [[The League|the League]]. Funded by the Looking Glass. This gives access to a [[Ministry|ministry]] that produces [[Winter|winter]] [[Vis|vis]] and [[Ritual|ritual]] staves.
## Overview
* The motion allows the Rebeshi Players of [[Holberg]] to cultivate the Blood Orchard in Holmauer Park (formerly known as ''Wunderkind Woods'').
* This [[Opportunity|opportunity]] was outlined in the [[Dear green space]] Wind of Fortune.
* Passed
## Date
* [[Autumn]] 384YE.
## [[Cost]]
* As detailed in the Wind of Fortune
* 32 Crowns
* Three months to complete

## Outcome
* Allows the Rebeshi Players to cultivate the Blood Orchard, and harvest both [[Vis]] and wood from the twisted trees.
* Creates the [[Tender of the Blood Orchard]] [[Imperial Title]] to liaise with the Rebeshi Players, and in return be able to purchase the valuable Winter [[Vis|vis]]. The title [[Will|will]] be appointed by unanimous decision of the senators of [[The League]].
