To create the position of [[Imperial Spymaster]], with the following powers: to [[Commission|commission]] a [[Spy network|spy network]] once per season; to decommission a [[Spy network|spy network]] once per season; to address [[Military Council]]. Their responsibilities [[Will|will]] be: to create, co-ordinate, manage and decommission [[The Empire|the Empire]]’s spy networks; to liaise and report to [[Senate]] and military council on matters to do with [[The Empire|the Empire]]’s spy networks. The position [[Will|will]] be appointed by Senate, held for one year, and revocable by the [[General]] [[Assembly]] and the [[Assembly]] of Nine.
''Proposed by [[Feroz]], seconded by [[Therunin]]''
### Overview
* Create a title within [[The Senate|the Senate]] with the power to address [[The Military|the Military]] Council, and both [[Commission]] and decommission a [[Spy network]] each season
* Passed
### Date
* [[Spring]] 383YE
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The [[Imperial Title]] [[Imperial Spymaster]] was created
* The [[Appointment|appointment]] took place at the [[Summer]] Solstive 383YE.
* Caleb of Felix’s Watch was appointed to the title.
