To create the [[Imperial Title|Imperial title]] of [[Dean of the Thorned Rose]]. A place of learning to be build just outside of Seren, in [[Miaren]]. This [[Will|will]] be a place to teach prospective vates. To explain what it means to swear the oath and to help those that wish to learn of [[Magic|magic]] and the [[Realms|realms]] in how to proceed. This [[Will|will]] function as a mana [[Sinecure|sinecure]]. The Dean [[Will|will]] be appointed by the [[Navarr]] Senators, and [[Will|will]] have [[Tenure|tenure]]. The Dean [[Will|will]] be expected to swear the following oath: “Never to sway from the path of the [[Vate]]: this I swear. Take in the willing, share my life’s [[Wisdom]]: this I swear. Train them in all they need to know, prepare them for the Oath: this I swear. Never to accept the Vate Oath of the irresponsible, reckless, or unworthy: this I swear.''Imperial Wayleave by Silvio di [[Tassato]]''
