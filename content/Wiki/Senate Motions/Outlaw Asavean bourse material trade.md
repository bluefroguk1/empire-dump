Assavian Blood Granite Bill
In that was as an Empire belive that the Soul should be free to follow virtue and that slavery is both against [[The Law|the law]] and a crime against virtue. We should endevour to use our power as senators to make sure that this truth, and the soul of [[The Empire|the Empire]], is not compromised by greed or other false virtues.
To that end
This motion is to make the trade of the following goods with [[Asavea]] illegal:
[[White granite|White Granite]]
[[Ilium]]
[[Weirwood]]

### Overview
* A change to [[Law|law]] regarding the trade of [[Bourse|bourse]] [[Materials|materials]] ([[White granite|white granite]], [[Mithril|mithril]], [[Weirwood|weirwood]], and [[Ilium]]), with citizens of the [[Asavean Archipelago]].
* Passed
### Date
* [[Autumn]] 379YE.
