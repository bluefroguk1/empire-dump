To create the title of [[Imperial Necromancer]]. The [[Imperial Necromancer]] has control of the Imperial [[Regio]] for the second half of the hour when it is possible to cast [[Whispers through the Black Gate|Whispers Through The Black Gate]], on each [[Day|day]] of each summit. They choose which covens and when may use the [[Regio|regio]] in this time. They are responsible for ensuring the [[Regio|regio]] at this time is used for the Needs of [[The Empire|the Empire]]. Appointed by [[Declaration]] of [[Candidacy]].
Proposed by [[Redoubt]], Seconded by [[Mitwold]]
