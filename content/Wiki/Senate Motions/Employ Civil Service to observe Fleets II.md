Put civil servants back on boats in order to prevent piracy.Proposed by [[Zenith]], seconded by [[Tassato]]
### Overview
* Piracy of foreign fleets is damaging relations with [[Foreign Nations|foreign nations]]
* [[The Senate]] has instructed the civil service to put observers on every Imperial [[Fleet|fleet]] to ensure compliance with [[The Law|the law]]
### Date
* [[Summer]] 379YE
### Upkeep
* 50 Thrones per season
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* Re-instates the earlier placement of civil servants on ships leaving Imperial ports
* The presence of a civil service observer on every [[Fleet|fleet]] in [[The Empire|the Empire]] makes it effectively impossible for PCs to engage in piracy of [[Foreign Nations|foreign nations]] or trade with [[Barbarian|barbarian]] nations. Consequently both options have been removed from the [[Downtime|downtime]] system immediately. 
* This measure was initally proposed during the Summer Solstice 378YE.
* It was then abrograted during the [[Spring]] Equinox 379YE summit.
