To build defenses at [[Holberg]]
''Proposed by [[Necropolis]], seconded by [[Karov]]''
### Overview
* Perform emergency repair of the gates and walls of [[Holberg]] based on plans presented to [[The League|the League]] by the engineer Rhiner Van Hesselman.
* Passed at 45 Thrones with 13 votes against
* Funds disbursed to Adamah of the [[Silent Tide|Silent tide]], [[Senator]] for [[Necropolis]].
### Date
* [[Spring]] 377YE
### [[Cost]]
* 100 crowns
* 25 wains [[White granite|white granite]]
* 10 wains [[Weirwood|weirwood]]
* 25 measures of [[Bladeroot]]
* 25 measures of [[Imperial Roseweald]].
### Progress
* Paid by Oksana Kazaku, [[Senator|senator]] for [[Temeschwar]] in Spring Equinox [[Downtime|downtime]]
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* Walls of Holberg restored to full strength.
The resources committed to the project allowed plans put forward by [[The League|the League]] engineer Rhiner Van Hesselman to be implemented. The defenses of Holberg were repaired to pre-siege levels, despite the fact that the [[Territory|territory]] was not under Imperial control at the time.
