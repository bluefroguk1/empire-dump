[[The Senate|The senate]] accepts the ceasefire proposed by the [[Sumaah Republic]] and welcomes a diplomatic delegation.Proposed by [[Karsk]], seconded by [[Hercynia]].
### Overview
* While [[The Empire|the Empire]] is not formally at [[War|war]] with the [[Sumaah Republic]], the Republic considers itself at [[War|war]] with [[The Empire|the Empire]].
* This motion recognises the [[Sumaah]] request for a ceasefire and welcomes a formal diplomatic envoy from the Republic.
* Passed
### Date
* [[Autumn]] 381YE
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The ceasefire begins at the end of the Autumn Equinox 381YE and lasts for a period of six months until the start of the [[Summer]] Solstice 382YE.
