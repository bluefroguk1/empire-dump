To develop the sinkhole for exclusive mining by a new [[Imperial Title]], [[Delver of the Depths]], to be auctioned on [[The Bourse|the Bourse]]. This [[Will|will]] provide a bounty of [[Weltsilver]] at a [[Cost|cost]] of zero wains, with an upkeep of 5 Thrones per season. This [[Will|will]] prevent access by private military captains.
Proposed by [[Miaren]], Seconded by [[Therunin]]. 
### Overview
* This motion was a result of the [[Opportunity|opportunity]] presented by the massive sinkhole that opened up in the north-western Empire.
* Passed
### Date
* [[Summer]] 381YE
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* [[The Senate]] has effectively assigned possession of the sinkhole to the [[Imperial Bourse]].
* The [[Imperial Title]] [[Delver of the Depths]] was created.
