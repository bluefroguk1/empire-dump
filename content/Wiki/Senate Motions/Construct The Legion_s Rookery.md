### Overview
* A [[Great work]] in [[Skarsind]] to take advantage of the [[Opportunity|opportunity]] that presented itself following the recent changes in Doctrine
* Passed
### Date
* [[Spring]] 381YE
### [[Cost]]
* 15 wains of [[White granite|white granite]]
* 5 wains of [[Weirwood|weirwood]]
* 5 thrones in labour costs
* 3 months to construct
