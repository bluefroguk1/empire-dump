That [[The Empire]] is at peace with the [[Thule]]
Administrative Motion 
### Overview
* Passed
* Due to the previous motion to establish a peace treaty with the Thule, it was unclear if the Empire had entered a state of negotiated non-hostility or if the Thule were to be considered foreigners. This motion was to clarify the stance of the Empire on the Thule.
### Date
* Spring 380YE
### Campaign Outcome
* The Thule are considered foreigners by the Empire, and as such it is entirely legal to trade with them.
