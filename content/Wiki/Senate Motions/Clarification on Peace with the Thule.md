That [[The Empire|the Empire]] is at peace with the [[Thule]]
[[Administrative motion|Administrative Motion]] 
### Overview
* Passed
* Due to the previous motion to establish a peace treaty with the [[Thule]], it was unclear if [[The Empire|the Empire]] had entered a state of negotiated non-hostility or if the [[Thule]] were to be considered foreigners. This motion was to clarify the stance of [[The Empire|the Empire]] on the [[Thule]].
### Date
* [[Spring]] 380YE
### [[Campaign outcome|Campaign Outcome]]
* The [[Thule]] are considered foreigners by [[The Empire|the Empire]], and as such it is entirely legal to trade with them.
