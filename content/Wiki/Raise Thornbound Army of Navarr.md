__NOTOC__
To commission the creation of the third Navarr army to be mustered in Miaren. The army to have the Thornbound quality. Self funded, to be created by the opportunity existing. Built by the will of the Navarr and the friendship and solidarity of the Empire. Virtue demands we turn words to action.''Proposed by Hercynia, seconded by Bastion''
## Overview
* This motion takes advantage of the [[opportunity]] [[Comes_a_time#Without_Fear_or_Favour|presented by the Navarr vates]].
* Passed in principle
## Date
* Summer 383YE
## Costs
* This commission uses [[Comes_a_time#Without_Fear_or_Favour|non-standard costs]]
* 200 wains of mithril
* 50 wains of weirwood
* 500 crowns
* The commission takes a year to complete
## Progress

## Outcome
* When complete, a third Navarr army with the ''[[Comes_a_time#Thornbound|Thornbound]]'' quality will be raised.
