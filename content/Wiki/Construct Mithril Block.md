__NOTOC__
To construct the mithril Block to ward the spires of Spiral.''Proposed by Spiral, seconded by Bastion.''
## Overview
* Commission The Block in [[Spiral#Cinion|Cinion]] as laid our by recent [[The_path_that_winds#The_Block|proposals]]
* Passed at 0 Thrones
## Date
* Winter 382YE
## Cost
* This commission uses [[The_path_that_winds#The_Block|non-standard costs]].
* 40 wains of mithril
* 80 crowns
* Three months to complete. 
## Progress
* 22 wains of mithril provided by Edmundo of Damakhan's Forge after the Winter Solstice 382YE.
* 18 wains of mithril and 10 thrones provided by  Maximilian after the Winter Solstice 382YE.

## Campaign Outcome
* When complete, the current penalties suffered to the production of [[mana site|mana sites]], [[congregation|congregations]], [[herb garden|herb gardens]], [[mine|mines]], and [[forest|forests]] owned those living in Spiral would be removed. 
* Furthermore, the protection offered by the block would mean that people would be feasibly able to live in Spiral despite the curse of the Black Plateau, allowing for a slight increase in the rate of recovery for the territory and its contributions to Imperial coffers.
