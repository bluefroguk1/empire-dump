__NOTOC__
Motion to support the Faraden expansion of the port of Caitun. This expansion will allow fleets to trade with Caitun and hunt monsters in the Sea of Snow. We shall provide the Faraden with 15 wains of weirwood to support this work at an expense of 45 crowns in transport costs. I will be seeking funds.
## Overview
* Supports the expansion of the Faraden port of [[Sea_of_Snow#Pelabuhan|Caitun]], as detailed in the [[Butterfly_in_reverse#A_Port_in_a_Storm|Butterfly in reverse]] wind of fortune
* Passed at 70 thrones

## Date
* Spring 385YE
## Cost
* Costs as per wind of fortune
* 15 wains of weirwood
* 45 crowns
* Six months to finish expansion

## Progress

## Outcome
* Expansion of the port of Caitun has been completed. Imperial [[fleet|fleets]] will be able to trade at the port and Wintermark, Marcher and Freeborn fleets will be able to hunt monsters in the Sea of Snow following the Autumn Equinox 385YE
