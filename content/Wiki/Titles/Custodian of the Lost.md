## Overview
The [[Custodian of the Lost]] is an [[Imperial Title]] appointed by the Conclave. 
## Responsibilities
The Custodian of the Lost has a responsibility to help people harmed by Magical Curses or by exposure to celestial forces.
## Powers
### Bequests
The Custodian of the Lost receives 108 rings each season from bequests made by the families of former patients.
## [[Appointment]]
The appointment, replacement, or removal of the Custodian of the Lost is made by the [[Declaration]] of [[Candidacy]] as part of a [[Conclave session]]. Any Imperial citizen may hold the title.
The Custodian of the Lost serves until they are replaced by another magician using the Declaration of Candidacy. As a title in the Conclave, they cannot be revoked.
