## Overview
The [[Ambassador to the Sumaah Republic]] is an [[Imperial Title]]. It is an Imperial position appointed by the [[Imperial Senate]]. 
After a false start in [[Winter]] 378YE, [[The Senate|the Senate]] eventually commissioned an [[Embassy]] for the [[Sumaah Republic]] in [[Spring]] 379YE. The project was been dogged by problems, and not completed until shortly before the Spring equinox 380YE. This delay exacerbated the growing fury of the [[Sumaah Republic]] towards [[The Empire|the Empire]], which culminated in a [[Declaration|declaration]] of [[War|war]] by the Republic in late 379YE.
That [[Declaration|declaration]] was rescinded during the [[Summer]] Solstice 382YE, but at the same time the [[Sumaah]] declared themselves the custodians of [[The Way|the Way]] and denounced Imperial claims to authority over the religion.

## Responsibilities
The [[Ambassador]]  is expected to represent [[The Empire|the Empire]] in relations with the [[Sumaah]] Republic. They have all the responsibilities common to an Imperial [[Ambassador|ambassador]].
## Powers
### Imperial [[Ambassador to the Sumaah Republic]]
The title has all the common powers of an [[Ambassador|ambassador]]: they receive a briefing, can receive a formal delegation, and may prepare a treaty.
### [[Sumaah]] Merchants
The [[Embassy]] in [[Necropolis]] means that the [[Ambassador|ambassador]] enjoys the benefits of mercantile support from [[Traders|traders]] prepared to deal with the Imperial citizens. As a [[Ministry]], this allows them to purchase quantities of special [[Materials|materials]] at a set price. The [[Ambassador|ambassador]] spends their money and makes their choices during [[Downtime|downtime]] from their character page. Money is removed from, and resources are placed into, the character's inventory when the [[Downtime|downtime]] is processed.

## [[Appointment]]
The Ambassador to [[Sumaah]] is an Imperial position appointed by the [[Senate]]. Any Imperial citizen can hold this title, and the ambassador serves for a year. They can be revoked by the [[General]] [[Assembly]] and by the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
