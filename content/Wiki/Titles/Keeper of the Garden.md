## Overview
The [[Keeper of the Garden]] is an [[Imperial Title]] that grants custodianship of a [[Sinecure]], requiring minimal responsibility but offering a regular income of [[Liao]]. Construction was proposed to [[The Senate|the Senate]] by Cesare Sanguineo Rezia Di [[Tassato]], in his position as [[Senator]] for [[Tassato]] during the [[Spring]] Equinox 385YE. [[The Garden]] of [[Glory]] is deep in the woods of Lower Tarn Valley, [[Therunin]].
## Responsibilities
The [[Keeper of the Garden]] has a responsibility to protect [[The Garden|the Garden]] of [[Glory]]. If [[The Garden|the Garden]] were to be threatened in any way, it would be the responsibility of the Keeper to deal with those threats.
## Powers
### Offer of [[Liao|liao]]
The workshop at [[The Garden|the Garden]] of [[Glory]] produces twelve doses of [[Liao|liao]] each season. The Keeper receives six of these doses. The other six are the property of the briars who reside in Lower Tarn Valley.
## [[Appointment]]
The [[Keeper of the Garden]] is a national position which is appointed by [[Judgement]] of the [[Navarr]] National [[Assembly]]. Only a citizen of [[Navarr]] may hold the title.
The Keeper has [[Tenure]] and serves until they die or step down. They can be revoked by the [[General]] [[Assembly]], the [[National Assembly]] of [[Navarr]], and the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
