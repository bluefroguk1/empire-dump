## Overview
The [[Ambassador to Asavea]] is an [[Imperial Title]]. It is an Imperial position appointed by the [[Imperial Senate]]. The [[Embassy]] that created the title was [[Commission|commissioned]] by the Senate in [[Construct Asavean Embassy|Winter 377YE]] following a serious diplomatic incident related to levels of Imperial piracy in Asavean territorial waters., but work was not completed until shortly before the Summer Solstice 378YE. Today the [[Sarvos#Asavean Embassy|Asavean embassy]] is a fixture of the [[Sarvos]] social whirl, and has led to an influx of Asavean traders which in turn has led to luxurious goods from the far west becoming more freely available on the streets of the city.

## Responsibilities
The [[Ambassador]] i[[Ambassador]]represent the Empire in relations with the [[Asavean Archipelago]]. They have all the responsibilities [[Ambassador#Responsibilities|common]] to an Imperial [[Ambassador|ambassador]].
## Powers
### Imperial [[Ambassador]] to the Asavean Archipelago
The title has all the [[Ambassador#Powers|common powers]] of an [[Ambassador|ambassador]]: they receive a [[Ambassador#Briefing|briefing]], can receive a formal [[Ambassador#Delegation|delegation]], and may prepare a [[Ambassador#Treaty|treaty]].

### Asavean Merchants
The [[Embassy]] in Sarvos means that the ambassador enjoys the benefits of [[Ambassador#Mercantile Support|mercantile support]] from traders keen to curry their favour. As a [[Ministry]], this allows them to purchase quantities of special materials at a set price. The ambassador spends their money and makes their choices during downtime from their character page. Money is removed from, and resources are placed into, the character's inventory when the downtime is processed.
Following the [[Ratify Liberty Pact|imposition]] of [[Tariffs#Sanctions|sanctions]] on the [[Asavean Archipelago]] during the Winter Solstice 381YE, the costs for benefiting from this ministry increased by roughly a fifth.-->
## Appointment
The [[Ambassador to Asavea]] is an Imperial position [[Appointments by the Senate#Imperial Position|appointed by the Senate]]. Any Imperial citizen can hold this title, and the ambassador serves for a year. They can be [[Revocation|revoked]] by the [[Assembly#General Assembly|General Assembly]] and by the [[Assembly#Assembly of the Nine|Assembly of the Nine]].
