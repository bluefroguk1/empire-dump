## Overview
The [[Speaker of the Magpies]] is an [[Imperial Title]] [[385YE Summer Solstice Senate sessions#Create title of Speaker of the Magpies|created]] by the [[Imperial Senate]] in Summer 385YE. The [[Speaker of the Magpies]] is recognised as the primary liaison for communication with the body of artefact researchers known as the Magpies. The title was originally created as a position with tenure and appointed by the Imperial Senate, this was [[385YE Autumn Equinox Senate sessions#Amend the Speaker of the Magpies|amended]] to a yearly appointment by the Assembly of Vigilance.
## Responsibilities
The [[Speaker of the Magpies]] has no particular responsibilities; they serve as representative of the Magpies, and are expected to make themselves available if any of the five Imperial structures wish to communicate with them.
## Appointment
The Speaker of the Magpies is a [[New Imperial titles#The Imperial Synod|virtue position]] which is [[Appointment|appointed]] by [[Judgement]] of the [[Assembly#The Virtue Assemblies|Vigilance assembly]]. Any Imperial citizen may hold the title.
The title can be held by any Imperial citizen. They can be [[Revocation|revoked]] by the [[Assembly#General Assembly|General Assembly]], the Assembly of Vigilance, and the [[Assembly#Assembly of the Nine|Assembly of the Nine]].
