## Overview
The [[Speaker of the Magpies]] is an [[Imperial Title]] created by the [[Imperial Senate]] in Summer 385YE. The Speaker of the Magpies is recognised as the primary liaison for communication with the body of artefact researchers known as the Magpies. The title was originally created as a position with tenure and appointed by the Imperial Senate, this was aMended to a yEarly [[Appointment]] by the [[Assembly]] of [[Vigilance]].
## Responsibilities
The Speaker of the Magpies has no particular responsibilities; they serve as representative of the Magpies, and are expected to make themselves available if any of the five Imperial structures wish to communicate with them.
## Appointment
The Speaker of the Magpies is a virtue position which is appointed by [[Judgement]] of the Vigilance assembly. Any Imperial citizen may hold the title.
The title can be held by any Imperial citizen. They can be revoked by the [[General]] Assembly, the Assembly of Vigilance, and the Assembly of the Nine.
