## Overview
The [[Speaker of the Magpies]] is an [[Imperial Title]] created by the [[Imperial Senate]] in [[Summer]] 385YE. The [[Speaker of the Magpies]] is recognised as the primary liaison for communication with the body of artefact researchers known as the Magpies. The title was originally created as a position with [[Tenure|tenure]] and appointed by the [[Imperial Senate]], this was amended to a yearly [[Appointment|appointment]] by the [[Assembly]] of [[Vigilance]].
## Responsibilities
The Speaker of the Magpies has no particular responsibilities; they serve as representative of the Magpies, and are expected to make themselves available if any of the five Imperial structures wish to communicate with them.
## [[Appointment]]
The Speaker of the Magpies is a virtue position which is appointed by [[Judgement]] of the [[Vigilance]] [[Assembly|assembly]]. Any Imperial citizen may hold the title.
The title can be held by any Imperial citizen. They can be revoked by the [[General]] Assembly, the Assembly of Vigilance, and the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
