## Overview
The [[Mirrored Maschieri]] is an [[Imperial Title]] created by the [[Imperial Senate]] as a position at the House Bourné School of Couture. 
## Responsibilities
The [[Mirrored Maschieri]] is expected to act as a champion of fashion and design right across [[The Empire|the Empire]], to celebrate true beauty and to support [[Sarvos]]’ future.
==[[Appointment]]== 
The [[Mirrored Maschieri]] is appointed by unanimous decision of the senators of [[The League]]. If a unanimous decision cannot be reached, the title may be appointed by the [[Imperial Senate]] instead. 
The title can only be held by a citizen of [[The League|the League]]. The [[Mirrored Maschieri]] has [[Tenure]], and serves until they die or step down. They can be revoked by the [[General]] [[Assembly]], the [[League]] [[National Assembly]], and by the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
