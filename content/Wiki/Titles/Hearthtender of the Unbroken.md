## Overview
The [[Hearthtender of the Unbroken]] is a [[Wintermark]] [[Imperial Title]] commissioned by the [[Imperial Senate]] during the [[Autumn]] Equinox 384YE. The position of Hearthtender is a [[Sinecure]], requiring minimal responsibility but offering a regular income of mana crystals. 
## Responsibilities
The Hearthtender has a responsibility to help those that have endured slavery and to rehabilitate them into [[The Way|the Way]] and [[The Empire|the Empire]]. In addition if the Hall were to be threatened in any way, it would be the responsibility of the Hearthtender to deal with those threats.
## Powers
### Bounty of Mana
The [[Hearthtender of the Unbroken]] gains custodianship of a [[Sinecure]]. They receive an income of 10 mana crystals each season.
==[[Appointment]]== 
The [[Hearthtender of the Unbroken]] is appointed by unanimous decision of the [[Wintermark]] senators. If a unanimous decision cannot be reached, the title may be appointed by the [[Imperial Senate]] instead. 
The title can be held by a [[Wintermark]] citizen. The Hearthtender has [[Tenure]], and serves until they die or step down. They can be revoked by the [[General]] [[Assembly]], the [[Wintermark]] [[National Assembly]], and by the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
## Hall of the Unbroken
The Hall of the Unbroken is actually a series of buildings dotted around the hot pools that occur in the East Floes. A place of rest and healing, the waters are often used as part of the process of helping visitors. Carved into [[The Door|the door]] of each building are the words "''Welcome to the Hall of the Unbroken, part of the Proffered Hand, the work of Lady Eleanor Novarion''". 
The largest of the buildings has a series of [[Mithril]] pillars designed to direct the flows of [[Magic|magic]] into strategically placed rare salts. Above the lintel of the main door the words "''For [[The Empire|the Empire]] and in honour of Brienne Stormborn and Tyr Brenna''" are etched and flanked by engravings of the [[Rune|rune]] of wealth.
