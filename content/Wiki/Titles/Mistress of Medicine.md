## Overview
The [[Mistress of Medicine]] is an [[Imperial Title]] [[384YE Summer Solstice Senate sessions#Construct Angelica Memorial House of Healing|commissioned]] by the [[Imperial Senate]] in Summer 384YE. The position of [[Mistress of Medicine]] is a [[Sinecure]], requiring minimal responsibility but offering a regular income of valuable herbs.
The Angelica Vergo di Sarvos Memorial House of Healing is in the region of [[Sarvos#Riposi|Riposi]], [[Sarvos]].
## Responsibilities
The [[Mistress of Medicine]] has a responsibility to support the national block of the League in excursions through the Sentinel Gate. If the House of Healing were to be threatened in any way, it would be the responsibility of the Mistress to deal with those threats.  Likewise, if any [[Opportunity]] relating to the House presented itself, they would make any decisions needed to take advantage of it.
## Powers
### Bounty of Herbs
The Mistress of Medicine receives an income of 18 drams of [[Cerulean Mazzarine|cerulean mazzarine]] each season from the Angelica Vergo di Sarvos Memorial House of Healing.
## Appointment
The Mistress of Medicine is selected by unanimous decision of the [[The League]] [[General|generals]]. If a unanimous decision cannot be reached, the title may be appointed by the [[Imperial Military Council]] instead.
The title can be held by any League citizen. The Mistress has [[Tenure]], and serves until they die or step down. They can be [[Revocation|revoked]] by the [[Assembly#General Assembly|General Assembly]], the League [[Assembly#The National Assemblies|National Assembly]], and by the [[Assembly#Assembly of the Nine|Assembly of the Nine]].
