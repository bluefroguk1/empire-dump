## Overview
The construction of the Azure Chorus was commissioned by Lorenzo “La Volpe” Macelliao von [[Temeschwar]] as the [[Bearer of an Imperial Wayleave]] during the [[Spring]] Equinox 382YE. Built at Braydon's Jasse in [[Kahraman]], work was completed shortly before the [[Summer]] Solstice of the same year.
## Responsibilities
The [[Azure Sutannir]] is responsible for the safety and wellbeing of those who visit the beautiful Azure Chorus. 
## Powers
### A Portion of [[Liao]]
The [[Sinecure|sinecure]] provides 6 measures of [[Liao]] to the [[Sutannir]] each season, to be used as they see fit. 
### Voice in [[The Synod|the Synod]]
The [[Azure Sutannir]] may sit in the [[Imperial Synod]], and possesses 12 votes in [[Synod]] affairs (or an additional 12 votes if they already have a [[Congregation]]). They may use these votes in any [[Assembly|assembly]] for which they are eligible. 
## [[Appointment]]
The Azure [[Sutannir]] is a national position appointed by the Synod. It is elected by [[Judgement]] of the [[Freeborn]] [[Assembly]]. . 
The title can be held by any Freeborn citizen. The [[Sutannir]] has [[Tenure|tenure]] and serves until they die or step down. They can be revoked by the [[General]] [[Assembly]], [[The Brass Coast|the Brass Coast]] [[National Assembly]], and by the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
## Azure Chorus
A beautiful temple of [[White granite|white granite]] and marble flecked with deep blue veins, the Azure Chorus stands not far from Fort Braydon on the foothills of the Gilded Peak. Below the temple is a natural amphitheatre, augmented with carefully sculpted blocks of [[White granite|White Granite]] to produce a curved, acoustically vibrant performance space, surrounded by tiers of seating with long sweeping access points for ease of audience participation. Performers are encouraged to demonstrate their [[Skills|skills]], the acoustics not only being superb for the singing but also encourage others to join in.
