## Overview
The [[Steward of the Nabarr]] is an [[Imperial Title]] created by [[The Senate|the Senate]] in [[Summer]] 383YE. The position of [[Steward]] is a [[Sinecure]], producing [[Bladeroot]] each season.
## Responsibilities
The [[Steward of the Nabarr]] has no particular responsibilities, they simply receive an income of valuable [[Herbs|herbs]] each season. If the [[Sinecure|sinecure]] were to be threatened in any way, it would be the responsibility of the [[Steward]] to deal with those threats. 
## Powers
### Bounty of [[Bladeroot]]
The [[Steward of the Nabarr]] gains custodianship of a [[Sinecure]]. Each season they receive 18 drams of [[Bladeroot|bladeroot]].
## [[Appointment]]
The title is selected by unanimous decision of the [[Navarr]] senators at the [[Autumn]] Equinox. If a unanimous decision cannot be reached, the title may be appointed by the [[Imperial Senate]] instead.
The title can be held by any [[Navarr]] citizen. The [[Steward]] serves until the next scheduled election. They can be revoked by the [[General]] [[Assembly]], the [[Navarr]] [[National Assembly]], and by the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
