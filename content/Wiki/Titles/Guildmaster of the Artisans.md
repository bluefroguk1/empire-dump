## Overview
The [[Guildmaster of the Artisans]] is an [[Imperial Title]] originally created by the [[Imperial Senate]] in Summer 385YE. Originally created as a position with tenure it was aMended in an administrative motion to instead be elected each year.
## Responsibilities
The Guildmaster of the Artisans has no particular responsibilities. If the Guildhouse of the Artisans were to be threatened in any way, it would be the responsibility of the Guildmaster to deal with those threats.
## Powers
### Bounty of the Hall
The [[Sinecure]] provides two measures each of [[Ambergelt]], beggar's lye, Dragonbone, and [[Iridescent gloaming]] and two ingots each of Green iron, Orichalcum, Tempest jade, and Weltsilver to the Guildmaster each season, to be used as they see fit. 
## [[Appointment]]
The Guildmaster of the Artisans is an Imperial position appointed by the Senate. Any Imperial citizen can hold this title. The Guildmaster of the Artisans serves for a year, and they can be revoked by the [[General]] [[Assembly]] and by the Assembly of the Nine.
