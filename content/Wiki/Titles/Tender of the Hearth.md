## Overview
The construction of the Hearth of Virtue was commissioned by ''Nikolovich Drakov'' as the [[Bearer of an Imperial Wayleave]] during the [[Winter]] Solstice 382YE. Built in Perumaki, [[Miekarova]], work was completed shortly before the [[Spring]] Equinox 383YE.
## Responsibilities
The [[Tender of the Hearth]] is responsible for the safety and wellbeing of those who visit, and study, at the seminary. 
## Powers
### A Portion of [[Liao]]
The [[Sinecure|sinecure]] provides 9 measures of [[Liao]] to the Tender each season, to be used as they see fit. 
### Voice in [[The Synod|the Synod]]
The [[Tender of the Hearth]] may sit in the [[Imperial Synod]], and possesses 18 votes in [[Synod]] affairs (or an additional 18 votes if they already have a [[Congregation]]). They may use these votes in any [[Assembly|assembly]] for which they are eligible.
## [[Appointment]]
The Tender of the Hearth is a national position appointed by the Synod. It is elected by [[Judgement]] of the Varushkan [[Assembly]]. 
The title can be held by any citizen of [[Varushka]]. The Tender has [[Tenure]] and serves until they die or step down. They can be revoked by the [[General]] Assembly, the Varushkan [[National Assembly]], and by the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
## Hearth of Virtue
The Hearth of Virtue stands in the Vale of Strascovia next to a temple dedicated to the virtue of [[Wisdom]]. The Hearth of Virtue serves as a seminary school. Within it has several classrooms and dormitories for people to learn from [[Storytellers|storytellers]] about the Paragons, the Exemplars, and the mysteries of [[The Way|the Way]] of Virtue. There is a central library, which incorporates many of the writings held in the [[Anvil]] Library.
There are seven smaller chapels within the seminary, each dedicated to one of the virtues. At the centre of each chapel stands a hearth fire, which copies the design of the national hearth fire on show during [[Anvil]] summits. Each chapel has symbolism throughout showing animals of the appropriate virtue.
