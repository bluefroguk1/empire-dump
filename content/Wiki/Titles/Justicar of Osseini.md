## Overview
The [[Justicar of Osseini]] was an [[Imperial Title]] created by [[The Senate|the Senate]] in [[Spring]] 384YE. The Justicar oversaw the [[Sanctuary]] of the Justicars on the Isle of the Osseini in [[Feroz]] which was home to a small community of [[Freeborn]] priests with a focus on [[Exorcism]] and dealing with the [[Unquiet dead|unquiet dead]]. The Sanctuary was destroyed by Asavean warships during the Spring Equinox 385YE
## Responsibilities
The Justicar was responsible for dealing with the [[Unquiet dead|unquiet dead]] and ghosts in [[The Brass Coast|the Brass Coast]], although the Freeborn are rarely troubled by such spirits.

The Justicar was able to purchase a variety of [[Magic Items|magic items]] and potions from the priests who worked at the Sanctuary with a focus on those that assist with the rite of [[Exorcism]]. The Justicar spent their money and made their choices during [[Downtime|downtime]] from their character page. Money was removed from, and resources were placed into, the character's inventory when the [[Downtime|downtime]] was processed.
## [[Appointment]]
This title was appointed during the [[Summer]] Solstice. The Justicar was appointed by a [[Judgement]] of [[Appointment]] in the Freeborn National [[Assembly|assembly]]. 
Any Freeborn citizen was able to hold the title. They could have been revoked by the [[General]] [[Assembly]], the Freeborn Assembly, and the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
