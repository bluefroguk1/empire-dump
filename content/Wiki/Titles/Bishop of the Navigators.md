## Overview
The [[Bishop of the Navigators]] (or ''Bisschop van de Zeevaarders'' to some of the Sarcophan) is an [[Imperial Title]] charged with maintaining good relations between the priests at the Temple of [[The Way|the Way]] in the [[Sarcophan Delves]], known as the Cathedral of [[The Navigators|the Navigators]], and the local populace.
## Responsibilities
[[The Bishop]] is responsible for supporting the Cathedral of [[The Navigators|the Navigators]] in Sarcophan, and maintaining the relationship between the [[Imperial Synod]] and the priests serving there. For the most part, this is likely to involve smoothing over any conflicts, and dealing with any problems of [[Opportunities|opportunities]] related to the Cathedral.
## Powers
### Point of Contact
Imperial priests serving in Sarcophan [[Will|will]] look to the [[Bishop]] to resolve any issues between themselves and the Sarcophan, in conjunction with the [[Ambassador to the Sarcophan Delves]] and the [[Assembly]] of the Nine.

## [[Appointment]]
This title is appointed during the [[Summer]] Solstice. The Liaison is appointed by a [[Judgement]] of [[Appointment]] in the [[General]] [[Assembly]]. 
Any Imperial citizen may hold this title. They can be revoked by the [[General Assembly]] and the [[Assembly of the Nine]].
## Cathedral of the Navigators
This massive [[White granite|white granite]] temple of [[The Way|the Way]] dominates the skyline of the Buitenlandsewijk, an area of the Delves where foreign investment is welcomed and encouraged. Seven tower-shrines, each dedicated to one of the virtues, surround a central [[Bastion|bastion]] where priests of the Way preach [[The Doctrines of the Faith]] to visiting pilgrims, and to local adherents. Each of the eight towers is topped with a dome of polished [[Mithril|mithril]] that catches the light of the rising and setting sun in a way that is described as both majestic and inspiring.
