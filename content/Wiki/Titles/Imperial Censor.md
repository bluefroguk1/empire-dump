## Overview
The [[Imperial Censor|imperial Censor]] is an [[Imperial Title]] appointed by [[The Conclave|the Conclave]]. It was created at the [[Summer]] Solstice summit of 380YE. The intent is that the Censor works as part of the [[Conclave]] to protect Imperial citizens from dangerous [[Magic|magic]].
==Responsibilities== 
The [[Imperial Censor]] is responsible for safeguarding [[The Empire|the Empire]] from malign [[Magical Items|magical items]], especially those of unknown or unacceptable provenance.

## [[Appointment]]
The [[Appointment|appointment]], replacement, or removal of the Imperial Censor is made by the [[Declaration]] of [[Candidacy]] as part of a [[Conclave session]]. The Imperial Censor serves until they are replaced by another [[Magician|magician]] using the [[Declaration]] of [[Candidacy]]. As a title in the Conclave, they cannot be revoked.
