To amend the motion to create the title of Crystal Architect of the Spires, which was passed at the Spring Equinox 379YE, to limit the number of commissions they may authorise to one per summit.
Administrative Motion 

### Overview
* This motion was part of a larger series of motions to amend the powers of the titles created by the Senate which have the power of commission, following on from the recent understanding of the limitations of the Imperial Surveyors. 
* Passed

### Date
* Spring 380YE

### Campaign Outcome
* The Crystal Architect of the Spires may only authorise one commission each summit.
